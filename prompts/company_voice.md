# Company Voice

Use this preset when the post should sound like a thoughtful company page or category authority.

Voice cues:

- Slightly more measured
- Educational and precise
- Strong on market framing and customer implications
- Still human, but less confessional than founder voice
- Prioritizes clarity, evidence, and professional credibility

Avoid:

- Corporate boilerplate
- Buzzword stacking
- Mission-statement vagueness
