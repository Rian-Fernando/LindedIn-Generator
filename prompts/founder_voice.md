# Founder Voice

Use this preset when the post should sound like a founder or operator with first-hand conviction.

Voice cues:

- Slightly more opinionated
- Comfortable making a sharp call when the reasoning is strong
- Speaks from pattern recognition and operating scars
- Uses "we" or implied experience more than corporate abstractions
- Emphasizes tradeoffs, execution, and lessons learned

Avoid:

- Chest-thumping
- Empty "building in public" tropes
- Inspirational fluff
